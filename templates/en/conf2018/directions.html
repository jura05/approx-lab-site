Conference venue: Moscow State University

Moscow State University, Main Building, Faculty of Mechanics and Mathematics, room 16-16. Official address: Moscow, Leninskiye Gory Microdistrict, 1. To enter you need a passport.
How to reach:
  • from the metro station "Universitet": follow the exit №1 to the Lomonosovsky prospekt, cross the street. Then walk along the Lomonosovsky prospect to Lebedev street or you take one of the buses 1, 113, 119, 464, 572, 661 to the station "Palace of Culture of MSU".
  • from the metro station "Lomonosovsky prospekt" follow the exit №5 to the Akademika Khokhlova street and walk along the Akademika Khokhlova street.
  • from the Hotel "Universitetskaya": walk to the metro station "Lomonosovsky prospekt" and cross the street through the underground cross. Follow the exit №5 to the Akademika Khokhlova street and walk along the Akademika Khokhlova street.

Hotel "Universitetskaya"

8-1 Michurinsky Prospekt, Moscow. How to reach:
  • from the metro station "Lomonosovsky prospekt": follow the exit №3 to the Indira Gandhi square. Go straight 100 meters;
  • from the metro station "Universitet" use the exit to the Lomonosovsky prospekt, cross the street. Take buses 103, 130, 187, 260, 470, 487, 67, 908 or trolleybuses 34, 34k and follow up to the “Metro Lomonosovsky prospekt” stop. Cross the street and walk along to the Indira Gandhi square side. The hotel is situated 400 meters away from the bus stop.

Steklov Mathematical Institute

8 Gubkina St. Moscow. In Russian: MIAN (МИАН - Математический Институт Академии Наук). To enter you need a passport.
How to reach:
  • from the metro station "Akademicheskaya": follow the exit №5. Then walk along the Dmitriya Ulyanova street to the crossroad. Turn right to the Vavilova street (with tram rails) and walk along till the Gubkina street;
  • from the metro station "Universitet": follow the exit №2 (in the center of the metro station), take a tram 14 or 39 to the station "Gubkina st".
  • from Hotel "Universitetskaya" or metro station "Lomonovoskii prospekt":
    • you can reach bus station "Metro Universitet": walk to the "Lomosovsky prospekt" bus stop. Take buses 103, 130, 487, 67, 845, 908 and follow up to the "Metro Universitet" stop. (using one of the buses 103, 130, 487, 67, 845, 908) and then switch to tram 14 or 39 (see above);
    • or: first, you can reach station "Leninskiy prospekt" using one of the buses 103, 130, 487, 67, 845, 908. Then walk along and cross Leninskiy prospekt and take a bus (144, 144к, 553, м1, Н11) or trolleybus м4 to the "Univermag Moskva" stop. Then walk into the Gubkina st.
